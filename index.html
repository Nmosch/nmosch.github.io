The Avengers

Earth's Mightiest Heroes

MARVEL

The Avengers, Earth's Mightiest Heroes, is a team of superheroes who's goal is to defend our planet from threats at home, aboard, and even into the cosmos. The team has had many forms over the years, but some of the most consistent members have been Captain America, Iron Man, Hulk, Hawkeye, Thor, and Black Widow. Others include Vision, Scarlet Witch, Ant-Man, Wasp, Doctor Strange and many others.
